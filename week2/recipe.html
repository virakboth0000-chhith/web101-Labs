Street Style Fried Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g fresh egg noodles
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup mixed veggies (carrot, cabbage)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Steps

  1. Boil noodles for 2 mins, drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large pan.
  3. Add garlic and stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  4. Add veggies and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Push veggies aside, crack egg and scramble.
  6. Add noodles, soy sauce, and oyster sauce.
  7. Toss everything for 2-3 minutes until hot.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your fried noodles!

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories Protein Fat
450 kcal 12g 15g
